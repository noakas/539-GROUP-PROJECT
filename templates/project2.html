Digititization:
William L. Clements Library

  • Digitize rare books, manuscripts, and graphics for HathiTrust and Clements' Image Bank

Since September 2015 I have been working at the Clements Library to digitize rare books for HathiTrust.

I am currently digitizing a collection of pre and post Civil War Slave Narratives.

Before starting, I spent several weeks setting up an ideal shooting enviornment. To shoot books we use a book cradle, two cameras, and LED lights. Each camera caputers a single page of the book.

I adjusted camera settings and generated camera profiles to ensure that LightRoom could accurately color calibrate images.
